Career & Skills Roadmap

Your guide to internships, placements, and building the right skills.

Placement Roadmap

Click on each year to see the recommended focus areas and action items.

Focus: Exploration & Foundation

  • Master a programming language (C++, Java, or Python).
  • Build a strong foundation in Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA).
  • Explore different technical domains (Web Dev, App Dev, ML).
  • Participate in college club events and workshops.
  • Create a basic LinkedIn profile and start connecting.

Focus: Skill Building & Internships

  • Solve DSA problems regularly on platforms like LeetCode.
  • Build 2-3 solid projects for your resume.
  • Learn about core subjects: OS, DBMS, and Computer Networks.
  • Actively apply for summer internships.
  • Start preparing for aptitude tests (Quantitative, Logical Reasoning).

Focus: Placements & Specialization

  • Revise all core subjects and DSA concepts thoroughly.
  • Give mock interviews and practice company-specific questions.
  • Polish your resume and tailor it for different job roles.
  • Stay updated on company visits and placement cell announcements.
  • Focus on communication skills and group discussion practice.
