How to Study Effectively

A simple, proven strategy for balancing college exams and skill development.

Do DSA Daily

Consistency is key. Solve at least 2-3 problems every day to build a strong habit and improve your problem-solving skills.

Mid-Sem Prep

Start your preparation 10 days before the exams. Focus on class notes and topics emphasized by your professors.

Finals Prep

Begin studying 20 days before your final exams. Prioritize past year papers and revise the entire syllabus thoroughly.

Attend Classes

Go to college daily. You'll learn which topics are most important for exams directly from the professors who set the papers.
