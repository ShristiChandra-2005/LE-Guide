Expression & Confidence

Finding your voice is a superpower. Here are some small steps to help you build confidence in expressing your ideas.

Practice in Low-Stakes Settings

Share your thoughts in smaller group discussions or with trusted friends before speaking up in a large lecture hall.

Start a Blog or Journal

Writing is a great way to organize your thoughts and become more comfortable with your own voice. It's for you, no one else has to see it.

Prepare One Question

Before a class or a talk, think of just one question you'd like to ask. Having it ready makes it easier to speak up.
